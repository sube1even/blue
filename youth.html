Children & Families

Christian Life Week (CLW)

Christian Life Week camps are aimed at high school aged young people. Operating in 7 different zones throughout South Australia, at different times during the year, the week-long camps offer a highly relational and activity focussed time of fellowship, worship and praise.

CLW is an important avenue of growth for the emerging church.

NOVO

A brand new state-wide camping initiative, NOVO brings together young Christians for a weekend of empowerment, faith and praise.

Live worship bands, guest speakers, in addition to small groups and time for personal reflection provide and exciting environment for young people to form and affirm their faith through copious amounts of fun!
