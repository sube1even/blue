Young Adults

Fuel

Fuel aims to energise young adults, the greater church and faith community by mobilising a young adult network so their experience of life and faith is enhanced and the church is strengthened. Highlighted by an annual weekend retreat, Fuel provides opportunity for young adults to continue growing in their faith by sharing experiences and each other’s company.
